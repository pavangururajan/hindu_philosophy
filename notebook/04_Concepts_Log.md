# Concepts Learned So Far

This document summarizes the core philosophical concepts, arguments, and frameworks Pavan has learned across the curriculum. It serves as a quick reference and review tool.

## Year 1: Foundations
* **Shruti vs. Smriti:** The distinction between eternal, revealed truths (Shruti/Vedas) and authored, traditional texts (Smriti/Epics, Puranas). Shruti is the ultimate authority.
* **Prasthanatrayi:** The three pillars of Vedanta philosophy—Upanishads (Shruti), Bhagavad Gita (Smriti), and Brahma Sutras (Nyaya/Logic). Any valid Vedanta school must be grounded in these three.
